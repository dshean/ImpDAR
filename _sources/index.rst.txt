Welcome to ImpDAR's documentation!
==================================


Requirements
------------

Python 2 or 3, 
`numpy <http://www.scipy.org>`_, 
`scipy <http://numpy.org>`_, 
`matplotlib <http://matplotlib.org>`_ 

To do anything involving geolocation, you will also need `GDAL <http://gdal.org>`_.
